History

Durham House of Pets was founded in 2002 by Julie Johns. She opened DHP to provide the community of Durham with a convenient place to buy what they need. In its early days, DHP sold only items pet owners need like food, toys, and shelter. Now, Durham House of Pets has several types of pets up for adoption to give them a home.

Why Use Us

Durham House of Pets is conveniently located on Main Street in Durham, New Hampshire. With no other pet stores within several miles of the area, DHP makes sure you have everything you need for every type of pet. Julie Johns has over 30 years of experience taking care of animals. She started her own business when she was younger, taking care of her clients pets while they were away. Julie has a passion for animals and wants to make sure every type of product is on hand to the community whenever they need it.
