    Delivery

    Delivery of products and/or animals is done within 3 to 5 business days from the date of inquiry. Items and/or animals will be put aside and packaged efficiently to make it to customers without damage and animals make it safely.

    To order items for delivery, please call the store at (603)667-9090 to speak to a sales associate.

    Return Policies

    In-store purchases

    For in-store purchases, with a receipt, customers have 30 days to return items for a full refund. Past 30 days or without a receipt, customers will receive store credit or must go through the manufacturer for a refund.

    Deliveries

    For deliveries you wish to return, they must be done in-store, with the receipt, within 30 days for a full refund. After 30 days or without a receipt, customers will receive store credit or must go through the manufacturer for a refund.
